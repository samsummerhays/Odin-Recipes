Vegetarian Enchilada Casserole

Description

This is a quick and easy vegetarian enchilada casserole recipe that anyone can make. As it only takes 6 ingredients and about 35 minutes to makes.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 10 (6 in) corn tortillas, cut into quarters
  • 1 (8 ounce) package sharp cheddar cheese, cubed
  • 1 purple onion, chopped
  • 1(3.8 ounce)can sliced black olives
  • 1(16 ounce)can green chile enchilada sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mexican cheese blend

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Spray an 8-inch baking dish with cooking spray
  2. Arrange 1/3 of the tortillas over the bottom of the prepared dish. Spread 1/2 the cheddar cheese, onion, and olives over tortillas. Spoon 1/3 of the green enchilada sauce and 1/3 of the mexican cheese on top. Repeat layering with the 1/3 tortillas and the remaining cheddar cheese, onion, and olives. Spoon another third of the sauce and mexican cheese on top. Finish with the remaining tortillas, sauce, and mexican cheese. Cover with aluminum.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes. Remove foil and continue baking until cheese is bubbly on top, about 10 more minutes.
