Pesto Pizza

Description

Basic vegetarian pizza that's good for those nights you don't have a whole lot of time to cook. Easy to tweak to make it your own special dish.

Ingredients

  • 1(12 inch)Pre-baked whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 1 cup prepared pesto
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped fresh spinach
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F
  2. Remove crust from wrapper and spread pesto over crust top; top with layer of spinach leaves, chopped tomato, shredded mozzarella cheese and feta cheese. Place pizza on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake pizza in the preheated oven until the spinach leaves are wilted and the cheese is melted, bubbling and beginning to brown, 10 to 12 minutes.
